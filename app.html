Help & support

Answers to common questions, and how to reach us.

How Proteva works

Proteva quietly watches over your loved one's device and stops most digital threats — scam pop-ups, phishing links, predatory apps, and remote-access attempts — automatically, before they ever reach you. For the rare situation Proteva can't fully resolve on its own, it flags it here on your dashboard with a plain-language explanation and what you can do. You're always informed, never overwhelmed.

Frequently asked questions

Does Proteva read my loved one's messages?＋
No. Proteva is noninvasive by design. It watches what the device is doing — like blocking a scam pop-up — not what your loved one is saying. It never reads private messages or tracks conversations.
Does my loved one know Proteva is installed?＋
Yes, always. Proteva is set up together with your loved one, with their full awareness. Protecting people with dignity — never behind their back — is core to who we are.
Can Proteva be removed?＋
Absolutely. If your loved one ever wants Proteva removed, the process is simple and immediate, and you'll be notified with calm, neutral language. We never make removal difficult.
How many people can I protect?＋
It depends on your plan: Home covers up to 2 people, Family up to 5, and Shield up to 10. You can change plans anytime as your family's needs grow.

Need a hand? Email us at hello@getproteva.com and a real person will get back to you. We're here to help your family feel safe.
